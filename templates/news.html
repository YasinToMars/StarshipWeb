Latest Starship News

7th Flight of Starship is scheduled for NET than Jan 15th. The launch window will open at 4:00 pm CT. In this Flight SpaceX will launch the new generation of Ship (S33) for the first time, which includes a new front section and forward flaps design, increased propellant capacity by 25%, vacuum jacketing of feed lines, new feed line system for Raptor-Vacuum engines, improved avionics and several other upgrades. The Ship will also fly with new tile experiments, including metallic tile options and one with active cooling. The Ship will attempt an in-space Raptor relight and deployment of 10 Starlink simulators for the first time. In this Flight, SpaceX will attempt to catch the Super Heavy booster (if tower and booster were in good condition). This Flight will follow the same trajectory as previous Flight, and the Ship will perform a simulated catch in the Indian Ocean.
